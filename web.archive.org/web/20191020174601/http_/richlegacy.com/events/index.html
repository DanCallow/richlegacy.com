Father Son Retreats

You only get 18 summers with your sons while they are still living with you. How will you spend this summer connecting with your son?

The families we work with through Rich Legacy have found the power of father-son and positive peer relationships to be more important than ever. There is an overwhelming negative influence boys (men-in-training) are experiencing online today. And it couldn’t come at a worse time. Dad’s now have an increasingly . limited amount of time to spend with their son, especially entrepreneurs and executives.
That’s why we focus on the QUALITY of time you spend with your son.
Afford your son the same benefits you receive from organizations you’re a member of like Genius Network, EO, or YPO. You understand the value of a strong peer-group and personal development, but do your children?
