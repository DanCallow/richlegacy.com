WHAT DOES RICH LEGACY BELIEVE?
We believe driven families have an undeniable need to pass their values onto their children, but struggle to find the tools, time, and consistency required. 

The resulting stress and distraction found when values don't align leads to unnecessary family conflicts and decreased performance.

Your investment in schools, tutors, teachers, and coaches demonstrate a clear commitment to providing your children with every opportunity to succeed. 

Nothing is left to chance. 

You know in your heart these investments mean little if your children do not possess certain values and life skills. 

Without this foundation, navigating the unique challenges found growing up in an affluent family during the digital age becomes very difficult. Your child's chances of success today, or 20 years from now go down dramatically.

Entitlement, laziness, decreasing academic performance, depression, anxiety, substance abuse, eating disorders, reckless spending, and other self-destructive behaviors begin to creep into even the best families. 

Unfortunately, the values and life skills you seek to instill in your children are rarely found outside the home in today's culture. 

That’s where we come in...

COUPLES EVENT
When your children don’t share your values, the resulting worry, time, and stress can negatively impact all areas of your life.

When kids are grateful, hard working, self-reliant, responsible, and resilient, not only do their lives improve, so do yours.

Event Overview 
  •   Why being a parent and a child is harder than ever
  •  What you can do to ensure your family's success 
  •  How to pass on values and life skills to your children
  •  Five Principles of Family Alignment:
  •  Connection - Reconnect and build trust by making time together more impactful
  •  Understanding - Increase understanding and respect through improved communication 
  •  Balancing - How to balance everyone’s needs and desires to reduce conflict
  •  Influencing - Power of influencing behavior versus making commands
  •  Empowering - Be helpful without enabling or preventing other’s growth

FAMILY WORKSHOP
You know what the next 12 months of your business looks like — but what about your family?

In addition to learning how to apply the Rich Legacy’s Five Principles, this half-day event allows you to connect with your family and your spouse while developing a family mission statement, goals and supporting activities to develop life skills, and character traits through this powerful workshop.

Children also have the opportunity to learn invaluable life skills to help them navigate life in and out of the home.


Event Overview 

In this workshop, parents and children will discover the value and how to:
  • Conduct family meetings
  • Set goals as individuals and as a family
  • Plan ahead
  • Utilize accountability partners
  • Practice active listening
  • Why being a parent and a child is harder than ever
  • What you can do to ensure your family's success
  • The importance of values and life skills
